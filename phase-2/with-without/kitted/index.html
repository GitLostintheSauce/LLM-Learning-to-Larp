Cognitive Profile — Self-Reflection Battery

Modeled on classic neuropsychological subtests (digit span, Stroop, trail-making, matrix reasoning, verbal fluency). This is a self-reflection tool, not a clinical evaluation — no population norms, no clinician. Read the results as a structured hint about yourself, not a diagnosis or a verdict on your worth.

What this measures

Five short tasks, ~8 minutes. Each samples one cognitive domain. At the end you get a profile, fitting career directions, and things to work on.

  1. Digit span — working memory
  2. Stroop — processing speed & inhibition
  3. Trail-making — attention & mental flexibility
  4. Pattern series — fluid reasoning
  5. Verbal fluency — idea generation
